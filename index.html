A Legacy of Noble Service

Founded in 2014, JGR College has firmly established itself as a beacon of traditional Islamic learning. Our intensive 5-7 year comprehensive course has proudly produced **over 50 or more distinguished graduates** who are now active, leading young scholars serving across various Islamic institutions and schools. Upon successful completion of this rigorous journey, students attain the prestigious, globally recognized **Alimiyyah Certification**, confirming their readiness to stand as reliable guides for the community.

Practical Teacher Training

Senior students participate in regular practice teaching lessons. Each of these training sessions is carefully monitored and guided directly by our beloved headteacher to perfect their delivery.

Yearly Progress Examinations

To maintain rigorous academic integrity, we conduct comprehensive yearly examinations that meticulously assess each student's textual assimilation and progress across all fields.

Our Inspirations

The pillars of wisdom and guides of righteousness who inspire our path of learning.

The Great Imam

Imam Abu Hanifa Al-Nu'man (رحمه الله)

A legendary Tabi'i blessed with matchless legal reasoning and absolute piety. He successfully structured the derivation framework of Islamic law directly from the Holy Quran and Sunnah, dedicating his life to preserving textual parameters against erratic interpretations.

Benefits to the Ummah: Formulated the Hanafi Madhhab, the most widely followed school of jurisprudence, streamlining judicial logic and structuring daily worship for hundreds of millions across the globe.

Prominent Students: Imam Abu Yusuf, Imam Muhammad al-Shaybani, Imam Zufar.
The Sultan of Saints

Shaykh Abdul Qadir Jilani (رحمه الله)

The pinnacle of spiritual purification and a master of Hanbali and Shafi'i law. He ran a world-renowned academy in Baghdad that combined internal purification with external legal expertise, transforming thousands of seekers.

Benefits to the Ummah: Revived true spirituality across the Ummah through the Qadiriyya order, strictly anchoring Tasawwuf (Sufism) to the pristine rules of the Shariah.

Prominent Students: Shaykh Ali al-Hiti, Shaykh Abu Umar Ibn Qudamah, Al-Hafiz Abdul Ghani Al-Maqdisi.
The Great Reviver

Imam Ahmed Raza Khan (رحمه الله)

The distinguished Mujaddid (Reviver) of the 14th Islamic Century, who authored over a thousand encyclopedic works across fifty distinct fields of sacred and secular science, defending traditional Sunni doctrine.

Benefits to the Ummah: Safeguarded the pure beliefs (Aqidah) of the Ahl al-Sunnah wal-Jama'ah in the subcontinent, anchoring modern practice to intense prophetic love and scholarly rigor.

Prominent Students: Hujjatul Islam Allamah Hamid Raza Khan, Mufti-e-Azam Hind Mustafa Raza Khan.
